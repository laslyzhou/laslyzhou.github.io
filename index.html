We are Aurobay

We’re a pioneering venture that brings together Volvo Cars’ powertrain business with Geely to develop and supply powertrain solutions for customers worldwide.

Inspired by our 150-year heritage, we aim to power the sustainable mobility of tomorrow, driving innovation, exploring new options and developing new technologies. Pushing the environmental impact towards zero.

Our vision is to re-imagine motion for a brighter future. We’re starting with the mobility industry. But it won’t stop there.
